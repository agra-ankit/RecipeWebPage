Jalebi

Ingredients

  • Milk powder, Maida, Ghee, Milk
  • Sugar syrup, Cardamom

Recipe

  1. Knead milk powder, maida, and ghee into a soft dough.
  2. Shape into small balls without cracks and fry on low heat.
  3. Soak fried balls in warm sugar syrup for 2 hours.

Lalmohan

Ingredients

  • Mawa (Khoya), Powdered sugar
  • Cardamom powder, Chopped nuts

Recipe

  1. Roast crumbled mawa on low heat for 5 minutes.
  2. Mix sugar, stir until the mixture thickens and leaves pan sides.
  3. Set in a greased tray, top with nuts, and cut into squares.

Rasgulla

Ingredients

  • Fresh Chenna (Paneer), Sugar, Water

Recipe

  1. Knead the fresh chenna thoroughly until completely smooth.
  2. Roll into small balls and boil them in thin sugar syrup.
  3. Cook covered for 15 minutes until they double in size.

Ladoo

Ingredients

  • Full cream milk, Basmati rice, Sugar
  • Saffron, Chopped almonds

Recipe

  1. Boil milk in a deep pan and add washed rice.
  2. Simmer on low heat, stirring occasionally until rice gets mushy.
  3. Add sugar, saffron, and nuts, then cook for 5 more minutes.
